A Fresh Start: Why and How I create my first blog

Why

Graduation is around the corner, the wind of December passed through my body, took away all the words that I have not dared to say and could not find the meaning to say. Though the road is still foggy ahead, I thought it's time to either embark on a new journey or watch everything rotting for the rest of my life.

How

For personal preference, GitHub Pages with plain HTML and CSS should be over-competent, the document is well-written and easy to follow, which leads to a quite smooth experience. I used GPT-4o mini to do most the coding for me. Without much prompt, the color theme and layout it output was basically all I wanted with few minor changes needed. The single frustration I faced was when I tried to add my LinkedIn account which got restricted after login and told me to upload my ID to recover. Haven't posted anything after registering and the only thing that could be wrong were my IP address and browser extensions, I still don't understand the point behind LinkedIn's anti-spam policy.
