A Fresh Start: Why and How I create my first blog

Why

Graduation is around the corner, implying the end of the first chapter of my life. The wind of December passes through my and carries away all the words that I have not dared to say or have yet to findd the meaning to say. Though the road ahead remains foggy, I believe it's time to either embark on a new journey or watch everything rot for the rest of my life.

How

For personal preference, GitHub Pages with plain HTML and CSS is more than sufficient, the document is well-written and easy to follow, which leads to a quite smooth experience. GPT-4o mini did most of the coding for me, and with minimal prompt, the generated color theme and layout was basically all I wanted, requiring only a few minor adjustment.

The only frustration I faced was when I tried to add my LinkedIn account, the accout got restricted after login and I was told to upload my ID to recover. I haven't posted anything since registering and the only things that could be wrong were my IP address and browser extensions. It's so difficult to understand the point behind LinkedIn's anti-spam policy.
